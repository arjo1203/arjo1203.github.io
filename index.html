Pan and Zoom with Paper.js

Extending Paper.js touch gestures to perform pan and zoom. I needed to add this functionality to Paper.js for my archtool_1.0 project, so that the user could zoom and pan around the terrain image they are surveying.

Multi-drawing with Paper.js

Extending Paper.js touch gestures to give multi-touch drawing functionality. It was a thought to add multi-drawing functionality to my archtool_1.0 project, so I made this concept to learn more about Paper.js and touch events.

Physics Simluator - Unique Experience Everytime!

When my simulator is loaded, 20 random objects are placed randomly in a space. With the laws of physics I learned at the University, I was able to create my very own physics engine. This allowed a more natural visualization of gravitational interaction. The idea of making object interact with each other came to me when working with Cannon.js. Since Cannon.js is built to do a varity of things (not one specific task) it was heavy on my system, forcing me to make my own distinct physics engine.

Watch a stable system!

Adding motion to cubes

20 cubes are placed in the center of a space, with little work I was able to intergrate THREE.js and Cannon.js. Cannon.js is a physics engine that is taking care of all motion in the scene.
