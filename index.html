Slices of the 4th Dimension!

I am creating this app for a Math Talk. The Math Talk will expand on the concept of the Conic Sections, where the Conic Sections can describe a higher dimensional shape, in this case a Cone. This app shows Quadric Surfaces, which are cross sections of a 4-D shape. By exposing ourselves as much as possible to these cross sections, could potential help describe a 4D shape!

Quadratic Surfaces

This app graphs Quadric Surfaces, there is a text field on top where you can enter a function of a Quadric Surface. The first 6 sliders rotate a 4D shape and the last slider shifts a hyper-plane in a dimension. The shapes we see are where the hyperplane intersects the 4D shape. Lastly, on the bottom there is another text field where you can enter a resolution to re-graph an interesting shape.

Physics Simluator - Unique Experience Everytime!

When my simulator is loaded, 20 random objects are placed randomly in a space. With the laws of physics I learned at the University, I was able to create my very own physics engine. Since Cannon.js is built to do a varity of things (not one specific task) it was heavy on my system, forcing me to make my own distinct physics engine.
